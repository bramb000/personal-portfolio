Hi, I’m Bramha Dalvi

I make digital entertainment and consumer products for global audiences.

I fuel teams with insight driven from understand data and talking to users so that we can craft products and features that solve real user needs, delight consumers, and are helps us sustain profitable businesses.

I currently work as a Senior Designer at Immutable, where I lead a team of UI and game designers to make some of the most enduring and meaningful web3 video games. I originally joined the organisation as a UX Designer on Gods Unchained, one of the oldest and most respected Web3 Trading Card Game, to bring the game to mobile.

During my time as a UX/UI Designer at Ubisoft I led the effort to imagine and build Rocksmith+ as truly multi-device experience, from PC to the latest game consoles to mobile and tablets, with a dedicated team of UI designers and engineers. This team was on a mission to push the envelope of what an accessible game means for a global audience and I was so proud to contribute to this mission as a colour blind human.

At Reliance Games, I brought entertainment for children to life as a UX and games designer. Partnering with animation studios such as Cartoon Network and Discovery Kids, I adapted popular children’s IPs into mobile video games and explored business models where majority revenue was generated via ads. Parallelly I also lead the revival of a beloved 5 year old cricket game to move its experience into a premium category to shift its target demographic.
